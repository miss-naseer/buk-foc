Faculty of computing in BUK?


The Faculty of Computing is a vibrant and dynamic community of scholars, researchers, and professionals dedicated to advancing the field of computing.
In BUK, the mission is to provide students with a comprehensive education, cutting-edge research opportunities, and innovative experiences that prepare them for successful careers in the rapidly evolving technology landscape.

The aim and vision is to be recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and innovation, producing graduates who are equipped to tackle complex technological challenges and create sustainable solutions.

Faculty of Computer Science and Information Technology in BUK was established in 2010. It is made up of four departments namely: Department of Computer Science, Department of Information Technology, Department of Software Engineering and the Department of Cyber Security. The faculty is committed to submission and delivery to local and international students excellent and satisfactory services. And has graduated her students with beautiful flying colors.


Departments in FoC:

Computer Science:
This is the study of the theoretical foundation of how computers work, including algorithms, programming, and data structures.
In BUK, The focus of the department is to lead in the advancement of computer science by equipping faculty and students with competency resources necessary to study and to prepare the students for a globalised technological society by equipping them with the knowledge and skills necessary to solve the complex technological problems of modern society.
Information Technology:
This is the practical use of computers to manage and store information.
In BUK, The Department of IT was established in 2012, but the undergraduate programmes commences fully in 2017/2018 Academic Session with the enrolment of the first set of undergraduate students into level 100 of the 4-year degree programme.
Software Engineering:
The creation of software applications that run on computers and devices. In BUK, The department is still young and fast growing. We currently run a single B.Sc Software Engineering programme, and hope to introduce more programmes, and PG programme soon are expected to be included soon.
Cyber Security:
Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, damage, or theft. Even though the department is under IT, it is still vital and the other departments need them equally.
